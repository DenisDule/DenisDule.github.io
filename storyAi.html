StoryAI

StoryAI is an artificial intelligence based story generator that me and 4 other group members developed for CS338. The generator uses the GPT2 library from HuggingFace in order to generate coherent text based on what is passed in. The generator stops as it is generating the story in order to prompt the user for input. This allows the user to steer the story into any direction that they wish!
